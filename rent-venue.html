Jogja National Museum

Jogja National Museum (JNM) is a contemporary art museum in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. JNM has established under Yayasan Yogyakarta Seni Nusantara (YYSN) management, JNM building complex was first a Former First Indonesia Visual Art School (ASRI-1950) and Faculty of Visual Art and Design (FSRD-1984) which later became Indonesian Institute of the Arts, Yogyakarta.

Taman Budaya Yogyakarta

Taman Budaya Yogyakarta (Cultural Park of Yogyakarta, popularly abbreviated as TBY) is a place of cultural development center in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. TBY complex consists of two buildings namely the Taman Budaya Concert Hall and Societet Militair Building. The Concert Hall main functions as official room for fine art exhibition, such as painting, visual art, sculpture, and craft.

Pendhapa Art Space

Pendhapa Art Space (PAS) is a masterpiece art space founded by Dunadi (sculptor) in Yogyakarta. This facility provides for the implementation of cultural and arts activities for the artists and the public. PAS is a complete art tourism destination where visitors can stay, create artwork, enjoy performing arts and exhibitions of paintings and sculptures, and even have discussions with the artists.

Malioboro

Jalan Malioboro (Javanese: , romanized: Dalan Maliyabara; English: Malioboro Street) is a major shopping street in Yogyakarta, Indonesia; the name is also used more generally for the neighborhood around the street. It lies northsouth axis in the line between Yogyakarta Kraton and Mount Merapi. This is in itself is significant to many of the local population, the northsouth orientation between the palace and the volcano being of importance.

Sleman City Hall

Sleman City Hall is a shopping center that offers a unique concept and is even different from other malls in Yogyakarta. The mall, which has the jargon Sleman City Hall - Pavvilion of Jogja, uses the word "pavvilion" itself which means "pendopo", where the "pendopo" itself is a gathering point for people to carry out various kinds of activities inside. Sleman City Hall itself has two very proud ballrooms, the Kamala Ballroom and the Malika Ballroom, where these two ballrooms make Sleman City Hall the only mall in Yogyakarta that has ballroom facilities.
